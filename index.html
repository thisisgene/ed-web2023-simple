Eugene Delta

Eugene Delta is the music project of Vienna-based artist Emanuel Donner. Their work explores the complexities of human experience through a unique blend of indie pop/rock. The latest single, Siren Call, released June 30th 2023, delves into the paradoxes of modern life, capturing the lure and strain of our digital age.

Eugene Delta's debut album, Calm Down, It's Over captivated audiences with its innovative storytelling and thought-provoking narratives. Expect a deeper exploration of these themes in the forthcoming release.
