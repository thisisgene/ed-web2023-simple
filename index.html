Eugene Delta

Eugene Delta is the music project of Vienna-based artist Emanuel Donner. Their work explores the complexities of human experience through a unique blend of indie pop/rock. The upcoming single, "Siren Call," releasing soon, delves into the paradoxes of modern life, capturing the lure and strain of our digital age.

Eugene Delta's debut album, Calm Down, It's Over (werk music 2021) captivated audiences with its innovative storytelling and thought-provoking narratives. Expect a deeper exploration of these themes in the forthcoming release.
